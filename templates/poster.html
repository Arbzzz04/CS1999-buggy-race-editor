A powerful web application for designing and customizing racing buggies

Features

Intuitive Interface

Easily navigate through various buggy components and customize their properties using a user-friendly interface.

Component Library

Access a vast library of pre-designed components such as wheels, chassis, engines, and more, to build your ideal racing buggy.

Customization

Customize the properties of each component, such as size, color, and material, to create a unique design.

Ease of Use

Use the drag-and-drop feature to easily add and remove components from your buggy design. As well as the autofill feature to quickly fill in the properties of each component.

Export and Share

Export your finalized buggy design as a downloadable file or share it with others, enabling collaboration and showcasing your creativity.
